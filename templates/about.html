About Plant Pulse

An AI-powered plant disease detection system built with MobileNetV2 and the New Plant Diseases Dataset, designed to help farmers and gardeners diagnose crop issues instantly.

eco Project Overview

Plant Pulse (formerly Ankur, meaning "sprout" in Hindi) is a full-stack web application that allows users to upload a leaf image and receive an instant AI diagnosis. The system identifies diseases across 38 plant-disease classes, classifying conditions like Septoria leaf spot, late blight, powdery mildew, and more — as well as confirming healthy plants.
